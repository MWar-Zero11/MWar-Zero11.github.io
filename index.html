About Me

I'm a passionate video editor with over 20 years of experience bringing stories to life through the power of visual media.

Multiple Sports Emmy Award-winning video editor and producer with over 20 years of experience crafting powerful stories for sports, entertainment, and corporate clients. Highly skilled in episodic editing, post-production workflows, and visual storytelling. Expert in Adobe Premiere, After Effects, DaVinci Resolve, and Avid. Proven ability to lead editorial teams, meet tight broadcast deadlines, and deliver compelling edits that resonate with audiences across platforms.

When I'm not in the editing room, you can find me riding my motorcycle through the Alps, playing frisbee with my Border Collie, Luna or experimenting with new visual techniques to enhance my craft.
