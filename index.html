About Me

I'm a passionate video editor with over 7 years of experience bringing stories to life through the power of visual media.

Based in Los Angeles, I've worked with brands, directors, and creators across a variety of industries to create compelling visual content that engages audiences and delivers results. My approach combines technical expertise with a keen eye for storytelling, ensuring that every frame serves the narrative and emotional core of the project.

When I'm not in the editing room, you can find me hiking the California trails, attending film festivals, or experimenting with new visual techniques to enhance my craft.
